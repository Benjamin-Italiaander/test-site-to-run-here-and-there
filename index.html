About

Based in Western Europe, I primarily collaborate with research institutes, universities, and government agencies across the Netherlands, Belgium, and Germany.

I help organizations take back ownership and control over their data by designing open-source-first platforms and sovereign hosting environments.

What I’m building

Over the past decade, major tech companies have accumulated vast amounts of data within their cloud platforms. More institutions are now weighing the risks: security, control, and ownership.

I’m developing a practical blueprint for building independent digital collaboration hubs (email, identity, enterprise apps, and infrastructure) with a strong focus on rollout strategy and operational reality.

I’m fortunate that CWI is giving me space to help realize this vision.
